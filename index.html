Comprehensive Survey Solutions

From underground utility detection to precision topographic mapping — our highly experienced team delivers survey data you can build on, anywhere in Oman and the Gulf.

Core Service
GPR Survey & Underground Utility Mapping
Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) is a non-destructive geophysical method using radar pulses to image the subsurface. Our team detects buried pipelines, cables, and voids with pinpoint accuracy.
What is a GPR Survey?GPR sends electromagnetic pulses into the ground and records reflections from subsurface objects. It produces a precise map of buried utilities — pipes, conduits, cables — without excavation. Critical for avoiding utility strikes and safe-dig planning.
Core Service
GPS Setting Out & Control Surveys
High-accuracy GPS/GNSS positioning for construction setting out, BM establishment, control networks, and large-scale corridor surveys across Oman’s diverse terrain.
What is a GPS Survey?GPS surveys use satellite-based positioning to establish precise coordinates for construction elements and control networks. We use RTK and static GPS to achieve centimetre-level accuracy — essential for roads, pipelines, and infrastructure projects.
Core Service
Total Station Surveys
Robotic total station technology delivering millimetre-precision angle and distance measurements for structural, engineering, and civil survey applications.
What is a Total Station Survey?A total station is an electronic theodolite with an integrated distance meter. It simultaneously measures angles and distances to compute precise 3D coordinates — ideal for building layout, road design, tunnels, and engineering control works.
Additional Services
Topographic Surveys
Terrain and feature mapping capturing contours, spot levels, and surface elements for engineering design and master planning using GPS and total station.
3D Laser Scanning
High-density point cloud capture of structures and facilities for as-built documentation, heritage recording, and BIM integration in oil & gas and construction projects.
Plot & Boundary Surveys
Precise land boundary demarcation and plot surveys for legal registration, title deed applications, and development approvals across Oman.
